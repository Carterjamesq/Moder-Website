Prospective Planning

coursFACTS provides both near and long-term visibility across courses and allows you to predictively plan your curriculum, enrollment, course schedules and faculty work assignments.

Faculty Planning

coursFACTS provides a simple way for users to schedule and manage faculty activities, teaching assignments, and professional workload throughout the academic year.

Interactive Visibility

By centralizing course and faculty plans, coursFACTS can automatically identify any discrepancies that exist and proactively alert the appropriate decision-maker. Any updates can then be made with ease.
